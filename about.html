About us

Our Company purpose

At Miners World in , our purpose is to responsibly extract and process gold through innovative elution techniques, while fostering a culture of sustainability, community engagement, and employee development. We prioritize environmental stewardship, ensuring eco-friendly practices and a minimal ecological footprint. This commitment to sustainability is integral to our operations, as we strive to minimize our impact on the environment while maximizing our social and economic benefits.

Our Vision Statement

“Unlocking the potential in the small to medium mining sector in order to help grow the economy in an environmentally sustainable way.”

Our Company Mission Statement

“To provide sustainable mining. Create employment and help maintain sustainable prices in the communities in the areas our businesses operate.”
