Elutions

Miners World offers comprehensive elution and smelting services to extract and refine precious metals from mining operations. Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with both furnaces and torches, allowing us to handle a wide range of elution and smelting requirements. Our team of experts utilizes advanced techniques and technologies to ensure efficient and precise extraction of gold and other valuable metals. Whether you need to process large volumes or handle specialized materials, you can trust Miners World to deliver high-quality results while adhering to strict safety and environmental standards.

Manufacturing boilers

At Miners World, we specialize in the manufacturing of high-quality boilers and CIP (Carbon-in-Pulp) plants for the mining industry. Our expertly crafted boilers are designed to meet the specific needs of our clients, ensuring efficient and reliable operation in even the most demanding environments. By supplying boilers and CIP plants, we help our clients optimize their gold extraction processes, improve efficiency, and reduce costs. With a focus on quality, safety, and customer satisfaction, we are committed to delivering exceptional products that meet the highest industry standards.

About us

Our Company Purpose

At Miners World in Manicaland, our purpose is to responsibly extract and process gold through innovative elution techniques, while fostering a culture of sustainability, community engagement, and employee development. We prioritize environmental stewardship, ensuring eco-friendly practices and a minimal ecological footprint. This commitment to sustainability is integral to our operations, as we strive to minimize our impact on the environment while maximizing our social and economic benefits.

Our Vision Statement

“Unlocking the potential in the small to medium mining sector in order to help grow the economy in an environmentally sustainable way.”

Our Company Mission Statement

“To provide sustainable mining. Create employment and help maintain sustainable prices in the communities in the areas our businesses operate.”

Coperate Social responsibility
