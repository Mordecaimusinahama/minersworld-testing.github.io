OUR SERVICES

Providing Miners with the best possible service

01

Elutions

Miners World offers comprehensive elution and smelting services to extract and refine precious metals from mining operations. Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with both furnaces and torches, allowing us to handle a wide range of elution and smelting requirements. Our team of experts utilizes advanced techniques and technologies to ensure efficient and precise extraction of gold and other valuable metals. Whether you need to process large volumes or handle specialized materials, you can trust Miners World to deliver high-quality results while adhering to strict safety and environmental standards.

02

Advisory services

At Miners World, we offer a wealth of expert knowledge spanning the entire mining value chain. Our team of experienced professionals has in-depth expertise in critical areas such as leaching, processing, and extraction. By sharing our knowledge and best practices, we empower our clients to optimize their operations, improve efficiency, and stay ahead of the curve in an ever-evolving industry. Whether you're looking to enhance your leaching processes, streamline your mining workflows, or explore new frontiers in sustainable extraction, our experts are here to guide you every step of the way.



03

Manufacturing/Supply

At Miners World, we specialize in the manufacturing/ supplying of high-quality boilers and CIP (Carbon-in-Pulp) plants for the mining industry. Our expertly crafted boilers are designed to meet the specific needs of our clients, ensuring efficient and reliable operation in even the most demanding environments. By supplying boilers and CIP plants, we help our clients optimize their gold extraction processes, improve efficiency, and reduce costs. With a focus on quality, safety, and customer satisfaction, we are committed to delivering exceptional products that meet the highest industry standards.


04

Mining Consumables

At Miners World, we understand the importance of reliable and efficient mining operations. That's why we offer a comprehensive range of mining consumables, including mining chemicals such as Caustic soda, Chinese and korean cyanide, Nitric acid, Sulphuric acid, HCL and Perxide and many more, at competitive prices. Our flexible payment options allow you to choose between cash and credit, ensuring that you can access the supplies you need without disrupting your operations. Whether you're looking to optimize your leaching processes, enhance your extraction methods, or streamline your mining workflows, our mining consumables are designed to support your success.
